About Yale Arte College

Founded in 1869, Yale Arte College has been at the forefront of art education for over a century. Our mission is to nurture creativity, encourage innovation, and inspire the next generation of artists.

  • State-of-the-art studios and facilities
  • Internationally acclaimed faculty
  • Collaborations with leading art institutions worldwide
