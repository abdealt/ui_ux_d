Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Sculpture

Explore the fundamentals of sculpture, from clay modeling to advanced stone carving. Hone your three-dimensional art skills and unleash your creativity.

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Sculpture program is designed to foster a deep understanding of three-dimensional art forms. Students will engage with a variety of materials, tools, and techniques, from traditional clay modeling to contemporary digital sculpting, developing both conceptual and technical expertise.

  • Hands-on experience in clay, wood, metal, and stone
  • Guidance from professional sculptors and artists
  • Integration of digital sculpting technologies
  • Collaborative and independent project opportunities
  • Access to state-of-the-art studios and equipment

Program Details

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Start Date: September 2024
  • Location: Yale Arte College Campus

Skills You Will Gain

Throughout the BFA in Sculpture program, students will acquire essential skills, including:

  • Mastery of traditional techniques: clay, wood, metal, and stone carving
  • Proficiency in contemporary practices: installation and mixed media
  • Digital sculpting with 3D modeling software
  • Conceptual development for three-dimensional art projects
  • Professional presentation and exhibition of sculptural works

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the BFA in Sculpture program are equipped to pursue careers in a variety of fields, including:

  • Professional Sculptor
  • Public Art Installation Artist
  • 3D Modeler for Film and Gaming Industries
  • Art Fabricator or Technician
  • Gallery Artist or Curator
  • Art Educator or Workshop Facilitator
