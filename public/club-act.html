Clubs & Activities

Get involved in a vibrant student community with a wide range of clubs, events, and activities to enrich your Yale Arte College experience.

Student Clubs

Our student clubs are a great way to meet like-minded peers, develop new skills, and take a break from your studies. Join any of the following clubs:

  • Art and Illustration Club: Collaborate on creative projects and exhibitions.
  • Photography Club: Capture and showcase campus life through a lens.
  • Drama Society: Produce and perform plays and short performances.
  • Film and Media Club: Create short films, documentaries, and animations.
  • Music Ensemble: Join students with a passion for music, from jazz to classical.
