Visit Our Campus

Experience Yale Arte College's inspiring campus firsthand and explore our state-of-the-art facilities.

Campus Overview

Our campus is located in the heart of New Haven, Connecticut, offering a dynamic and creative environment for artists and students. Join us for a campus tour to see our studios, galleries, and classrooms.

  • State-of-the-art art studios
  • Modern classrooms and workshops
  • Dedicated exhibition galleries
  • Collaborative spaces for creative work
  • On-campus cafes and student lounges

Plan Your Visit

Visitors are welcome Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We encourage you to schedule a guided tour to make the most of your visit.

  • Guided Campus Tours: Available by appointment.
  • Parking: Free parking available at designated visitor lots.
  • Contact Us: For inquiries, email us at visit@yale.edu or call (203) 432-4771.
