Admission Requirements

Below are the requirements to apply to Yale Arte College. Make sure to meet all the listed criteria before submitting your application.

Undergraduate Programs

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Completed application form
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)
  • Portfolio showcasing your artistic work
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Official transcripts

Admission Requirements

Ensure you meet the admission requirements to join Yale Arte College and kickstart your artistic journey.

Undergraduate Programs

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Completed application form
  • Personal statement (500-750 words)
  • Portfolio showcasing your artistic work
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Official transcripts

Graduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field
  • Completed application form
  • Statement of purpose (1000 words)
  • Advanced portfolio showcasing professional work
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Official undergraduate transcripts
  • CV or Resume

Additional Notes

International Applicants: Proof of English proficiency is required (TOEFL or IELTS scores). All documents must be officially translated into English.

Need more details? Visit our Admissions Overview page or contact our admissions office.
