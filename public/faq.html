Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Yale Arte College.

General Questions

Yale Arte College offers a variety of programs including Bachelor's and Master's degrees in fine arts, as well as short workshops and specialized courses.

You can apply by completing the online application form available on our Apply page and submitting the required documents.

The application deadline for undergraduate programs is September 15, and for graduate programs, it is November 1.

Financial Aid

Yes, Yale Arte College offers a variety of scholarships based on merit and financial need. Visit the Funding Options page for more details.

To apply for financial aid, you must submit a completed financial aid application along with proof of income. Details can be found in the Financial Aid Guide available on our Brochures & Documents page.

Campus Life

Yale Arte College offers a wide range of extracurricular activities including art clubs, music ensembles, drama societies, and sports teams. Learn more on the Clubs & Activities page.

Yes, on-campus housing is available for both undergraduate and graduate students. Our modern facilities ensure a comfortable living and learning experience.
